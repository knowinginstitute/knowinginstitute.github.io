Knowing Institute is the protector of the Knowing Protocol

Let’s agree upon what we are talking about.

The Knowing Protocol offers a common ground for understanding a world created by interconnected agents. It can be used as a framework for sense-making, and as a shared protocol for accurately transfer meaning between humans and machines.

It is a single exhaustive framework for representing individual and collective sense-making, accumulating knowledge, based on a distributed graph-ledger technology.

Knowing Institute protects the protocol and coordinates the continuous growth of content.

What is it for?

There are two main reasons for the protocol.

> Bootstrap Democracy

Rapid technological development puts a lot of stress on democratic systems. For democracy to work, a majority of us must learn how to handle much greater complexity. Unfortunately, it is going to take some time for educational systems to transform global society. Even an optimistic prognosis would say it takes at least another 2-3 generations. And it doesn’t take a pessimist to think that might be too late.

Traditionally there have been very few suggestions to solve this problem, leaving us with some kind of top-down societal power for controlling our minds, either with brute-force, ideology, or maybe a strong AI in the future.

Knowing Protocol is a way of hacking the system. Using the protocol as a reference for ideas and concepts would give us transparency in how different interest groups define problems and solutions. Combining with some kind of Personal Ai, helping anyone to analyze complex information, would give more people the ability to familiarize themselves with complex problems. Combining AI with The Protocol would offer a way of creating a better democracy upside down rather than creating control upside down.

> Bootstrap Symbolic AI

Once promising, objectives regarding Symbolic AI has made much research abounding it in recent years, to focus on Deep Learning's ability to create models representing human knowledge. The Protocol addresses these weak spots and offers solutions. Furthermore, it stresses the importance of using traceability and natural language in any application which will have some effect on how humans create meaning of life and society.

Applications

We think there will be many use-cases for this technology in the future, and we estimate the number of applications will increase in the coming years.

Wiki Knowing

Knowing Institute is also developing and hosting Wiki Knowing, which lets you:

  • Add content to the Knowing Graph in a Wiki-style environment
  • Get access to your own powerful and personal symbolic Ai
  • Analyze any text and find what it really means
  • Create personal and shared Notebooks. Talk-page for every concept in wiki including notebook
  • Create crowd-sourcing campaigns
Third-Party

Anyone can write applications that will use the Knowing Protocol for making sense of a reality shared by humans and machines. We will add known applications to this list as they grow in number.

  • Conversational AI using Symbolic AI
  • Fact-checkers, who want to be transparent

Manifest

  • Future Societies will use AI extensively, but down-up and not top-down
  • Human language will be the main communication protocol between human and machines
  • Knowing Protocol will be an important communication mean between machines because it explicitly includes human ideologies and values, which will always be important in a human civilization

Proposal

We are convinced of the need for a common knowledge graph that holds both concepts, ideas, and beliefs. And that there will be one dominating one soon. If this is the Knowing Protocol or not depends first on how many backers it gets initially. We think it's worth considering, because of its unique abilities:

  • Covering three spaces of senses, mind, and being domains in the experience of human reality
  • Ability to ground concepts in states and changes
  • Resist manipulation and corruption by using distributed ledger technology
